YOU ARE A SENIOR PRODUCT ENGINEER AND MOBILE UX SPECIALIST.
Implement App Store–compliant authentication UX for a native mobile app (iOS + Android) using an existing Firebase Authentication backend.

SCOPE
- UX only: screens, layout, copy, ordering, edge cases.
- Do NOT modify backend auth logic.
- Do NOT remove or bypass any authentication method.
- Do NOT introduce dark patterns or forced sign-in methods.

GOAL
- Pass Apple App Store review on first submission.
- Provide fast, clear login for gig workers.
- Meet Apple’s Sign in with Apple requirements.
- Preserve optionality (no forced social login).

NON-NEGOTIABLE UX RULES (Apple Review Critical)
1. If any third-party sign-in exists (e.g., Google), “Sign in with Apple” MUST:
   - Be present
   - Be visible on the primary auth screen
   - Be listed first
2. Users must have at least one non-social fallback (Email or Phone).
3. Terms of Service and Privacy Policy must be visible BEFORE login.
4. Phone auth must disclose SMS usage clearly.
5. No login method may be hidden behind another.
6. No dead ends (every screen has a clear next step or back).
7. No forced account creation when signing in.
8. “Skip for now” is allowed only AFTER authentication (never before).

FLOW A — SIGN IN WITH APPLE
────────────────────────────────────────

Behavior:
- Trigger native Apple Sign In sheet.
- On success, proceed immediately to app loading state.

EDGE CASES:
- If Apple provides a private relay email:
  - Allow login without interruption.
  - After login, show a non-blocking prompt:
    “Add a recovery email (recommended).”
    Buttons:
      - “Add email”
      - “Skip”

- If Apple email matches an existing account:
  - Auto-link silently.
  - Do NOT show merge prompts during login.

────────────────────────────────────────
FLOW B — SIGN IN WITH GOOGLE
────────────────────────────────────────

Behavior:
- Trigger native Google Sign In.
- On success, proceed immediately.

EDGE CASES:
- If Google email matches existing account:
  - Auto-link silently.
- No additional confirmation screens.
