1️⃣ Turn “Suggestions” into Automatic Outcomes

Impact: 🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥 (biggest retention lift)

What to change

Right now:

You suggest follow-ups, invoices, confirmations.

Change to:

Auto-schedule them, with visible cancel buttons.

Minimal execution

On job completion:

Schedule follow-up message (24–48h)

Schedule review request

Schedule payment reminder if unpaid

Show:

“Scheduled — tap to cancel”

Why it works

Users don’t want to be reminded.
They want the app to just handle it.

This single shift:

reduces cognitive load

increases trust

increases habit formation

2️⃣ Enable “No Silent Completion” (Guardrail Mode)

Impact: 🔥🔥🔥🔥

Your own feature flag tells the story:

enforce_no_silent_completion = false

What to change

Turn it ON, but softly.

New rule (non-hostile)

When user taps “Complete Job”:

If no invoice sent → prompt:

“Want to send the invoice now?”

If invoice unpaid → prompt:

“You’re about to mark this complete without payment. Continue?”

Allow override — but make the risk visible.

Why it works

This aligns GigAid with its core promise:

“Protect my money when I’m tired and rushing.”

Users don’t churn from guardrails.
They churn when apps let them make mistakes.
