---

## CONTEXT

You are implementing a **safe, event-driven client notification system** for a gig-worker app (GigAid).

This system allows gig workers to notify **existing clients only** when a **real-world event** materially changes relevance (weather, season, risk, availability, or client history).

This system is **not** a marketing tool.
Mass messaging, promotions, discounts, or arbitrary announcements are **explicitly prohibited**.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

1. Every notification **must be tied to a validated event**
2. Every message **must include a booking link**
3. Messages are **rare, time-bound, and informational**
4. AI may **suggest** campaigns but may **never send autonomously**
5. If an event cannot be justified → **block the send**
6. High-risk services have **stricter rules**

## PHASE 1 — USER-INITIATED EVENT NOTIFICATIONS

### ENTRY POINT

Button: **“Notify Clients”**

---

### REQUIRED USER INPUTS (ALL MANDATORY)

A notification **cannot be sent** unless all are provided:

* `service_id`
* `category`
* `event_type` (filtered by category)
* `event_reason` (max 120 characters)
* `audience_filter` (past clients only)
* `booking_link`
* `channel` (SMS or Email)

---

### MESSAGE CONSTRAINTS (STRICT)

**SMS**

* Max 320 characters
* Plain text only
* Max 1 emoji
* Must include: `Reply STOP to opt out`

**Email**

* Plain text or minimal HTML
* No images
* No marketing language

---

### DISALLOWED CONTENT (BLOCK SEND)

* Discounts or pricing promotions
* Percent-off language
* “Limited time offer”
* “New service announcement” without event
* Fear-based language unless `event_type === RISK`
* Repeated nudging

---

### RATE LIMITS (HARD ENFORCEMENT)

* Max **1 notification per service per 7 days**
* Max **2 notifications per account per week**
* No sends during quiet hours (server-time enforced)

---

## PHASE 2 — AI-SUGGESTED EVENT NOTIFICATIONS (ADVISORY ONLY)

### AI ROLE

AI is **advisory only**.

AI may:

* Detect seasonal or environmental signals
* Detect client inactivity or risk windows
* Suggest **at most one** notification

AI may **never**:

* Send messages
* Bypass validation
* Repeat a dismissed suggestion
* Suggest notifications for “Other” category

---

### AI SUGGESTION CONDITIONS (ALL REQUIRED)

AI may surface a suggestion only if:

1. A valid external or temporal signal exists
2. The service category allows the detected event
3. There are eligible past clients
4. No notification was sent for this service in last 7 days
5. Suggestion has not already been dismissed

If any check fails → **no suggestion**

---

### AI SUGGESTION UI COPY (FIXED STYLE)

> “An event affecting your clients was detected.
> Would you like to notify them?”

Include:

* Event detected
* Service name
* Rounded estimate of eligible clients
* CTA: **Review Draft**

No urgency language.
No metrics.
No pressure.
